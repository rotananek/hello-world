Wedding Overview

The wedding will be at Camp Friedenswald in Southern Michigan. It will be a weekend event on December 14th and 15th, 2019. The first day will be in a relaxed retreat style, and the second day will be the official ceremony and reception.

Lodging

We will provide housing in Friedenswald's all-season cabins that each hold 10-14 people on Saturday night. Please let us know in the RSVP if you need this so we can make reservations. There are limited rooms available in Sandhill Lodge as well. More lodging facility details

Plus Ones

Guests may bring a +1 who is a significant other or spouse.

Food

Meals will be provided: Saturday lunch and dinner, and Sunday breakfast and lunch.
